Our history

The Nooklyate team has been working together for several years focused on business intelligence, analytics, and machine learning. We are a network of individuals with backgrounds that span most industries and technologies. Our expertise is aligning data science and software technology to business needs. This can only be done well by leveraging experience and clear communication skills.

Dr. Patrick Weinkam has led the data science development efforts and coordinated teams of talented developers. He started his career in physics and biomedical software development where he developed web server software used for research in industry and academia. He then moved into the technology industry to work on a variety of data science applications, for example in the data center, energy, and manufacturing industries. He recently cofounded and is CTO of CYTK, a company developing mobile virtual assistant applications for the automotive industry.
